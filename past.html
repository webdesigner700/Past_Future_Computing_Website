The techology used for the kinect motion sensor was really intriguing. After this, I was hooked on to gaming and computing. When I was in my 8th grade I started to take computing subjects in my school and I really liked them. I took subjects which taught me the basics of computing hardware, software and the operating system. I also learnt a bit of HTML when I was in my 10th grade.
